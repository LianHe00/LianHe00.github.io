About Me
======

I received my bachelor's degree in **Computer Science and Technology** from the **School of Computer Science, Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT)**. I am currently in the **combined master's–doctoral program** at the School of Computer Science, BIT, advised by **Prof. Gangyi Ding**. I am studying at the **Beijing Zhongguancun Academy**, with research interests in **embodied AI** and **affordance perception**.

Feel free to explore my publications, talks, teaching, and CV via the navigation menu. I welcome academic collaboration and discussion.
